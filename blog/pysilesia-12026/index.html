PySilesia #1/2026

dodane przez Dominik Jon w dniu 2026-02-10

Prowadzący: Bartosz Sławecki

Tytuł: Programming Strategically

Opis: I once believed great software came from perfect APIs and a single universal style. That belief didn't survive contact with reality. Software, is more importantly, defined by usefulness, strategic thinking, difficult trade-offs, and human creativity.

Through the lens of CPython, I'll share a journey of fixes that weren't worth making, "brilliant" ideas that didn't cut it, and surprisingly simple edge cases in your code that you'll never unsee.

O prelegencie: Bartosz's career has always balanced two passions: building things as a software engineer and helping others grow as a technical trainer. He is also a member of the Python Triage Team. Bartosz co-organized PyCon PL 2025 and Rustmeet 2025. He cares deeply about open source, enjoys the history of mathematics, and aims to balance pragmatism with a philosophical and creative outlook on life.
